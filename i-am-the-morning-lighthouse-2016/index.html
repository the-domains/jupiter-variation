I Am the Morning - Lighthouse (2016)

Lighthouse was awarded Album of the Year 2016 in the Progressive Music Awards, I Am the Morning record for Kscope, Mariusz Duda from Riverside performs a duet in the self-titled song and normally all these facts should be enough for someone to understand that I Am the Morning play Prog Rock. However, that would be much too restricting and diminishing for the music of the Russian duo (Marjana Semkina in vocals and Gleb Kolyadin in piano) who in their third studio album (plus 1 EP and 1 live album) deliver some of the most beautiful music I have ever heard.

The album begins with I Came Before the Water pt1 which sets the tone for what is going to follow. The ethereal vocals of Semkina and the piano of Kolyadin provide a neoclassical foundation on top of which every song of the album is further built by incorporating Jazz and Rock elements. Expect no loud guitar moments though, the album has an introspective and melancholic tone which in combination with the lyrical context -which revolves around mental illness - successfully creates and delivers to the listener a completely integrated piece of art. Be warned that this is not an easy album. But it is majestic and wonderful and the more you expose yourself to it the more you can discover and gain. Together with Marillion they are the two top contestants for my progressive albums of the year list !
